About the Rivera Heart and
Metabolism Center
Rivera Heart & Metabolism Center Located in Panabo City, Davao del Norte, within Rivera Medical Center, Inc., Rivera Heart & Metabolism Center was established on December 16, 2022. We are a specialized facility dedicated to non-invasive diagnostics and personalized care in cardiovascular and metabolic health. Our expert team, comprising of cardiologists and an endocrinologist, offers comprehensive services in echocardiography, electrophysiological studies, vascular medicine, critical care, and metabolic disease management including diabetes and thyroid conditions. We are committed to providing professional treatment with compassion and integrity, striving to improve the well-being of our community through advanced medical practices and dedicated healthcare excellence. Our goal is to become a beacon of cardiometabolic innovation and healing in Mindanao, ensuring essential health services are always within reach.
Our Mission
To provide exceptional cardiometabolic care, merging advanced medical knowledge with compassion. Guided by a dedication to excellence and a deep belief in serving for God's greater glory, we pledge to elevate the well-being of Mindanao and set the standard in heart health.
Our Vision
Our center becomes the cornerstone of cardiometabolic innovation and healing in Mindanao. We will pioneer advancements in heart health, creating an environment where medical professionals thrive on integrity and empathy.
